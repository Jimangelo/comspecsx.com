Computer Hardware

The physical components that a computer device needs to operate are known as computer hardware. It includes anything that has to do with a circuit board in a computer or notebook.

Internal Hardware

A device that is installed in the computer is referred to as internal. (e.g., motherboard, power supply, video card, RAM etc.

External Hardware

A hardware unit that is mounted outside of the computer is referred to as external. (e.g.,monitor, keyboards, speaker, printer etc.)
