About ComSpecs X



Mission and History

Founded in April 2021 and was based in Camarines Sur, Philippines, ComSpecs X is a trusted community market place for computers parts and maintenance. The company was built by four (4) senior high school.

Our mission is to become a highly reliable and productive Computer Hardware Reseller company that makes small businesses much better through our products we’ve offered to you. Our company provides affordable and good quality products that will assist businesses in ensuring success and to be connected to our world and the current generation.

Goals and Objectives

1. To help businesses technologies to always in a good condition to assist their businesses.

2. To produce and develop a good-quality products, and inexpensive to be the best offer in computer hardware servicing.

3. To incorporate with social media marketing, to engage people by sharing a advantageous content of our products, and also to improve the customer servicing.

4. To increase the total income by 15% and to produce and launch different products over the next two whole years
5. The time frame for reaching our goals is 1-3 years to accomplish different goals.
